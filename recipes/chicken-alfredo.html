Chicken Alfredo

A creamy and delicious pasta dish made with tender chicken breast, rich Alfredo sauce, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the chicken breasts in a pan until fully cooked, then slice into strips.
  2. In the same pan, add butter and garlic. Saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add the heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  4. Stir in the Parmesan cheese until the sauce thickens.
  5. Mix in the chicken strips, then season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve over pasta and enjoy!
