Margherita Pizza

A simple yet flavorful Italian classic made with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, basil, and a drizzle of olive oil on a crispy, thin crust.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce or fresh crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes evenly over the dough.
  5. Arrange the sliced mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven and immediately add fresh basil leaves.
  8. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with a pinch of salt.
  9. Slice and serve your fresh Margherita Pizza!
