Tiramisu

A popular Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cheese, and cocoa.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack ladyfingers
  • 1 cup brewed coffee
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

Instructions

  1. Dip ladyfingers in coffee and place them in a baking dish.
  2. Mix mascarpone cheese with sugar and spread over the ladyfingers.
  3. Whip heavy cream and layer it over the mascarpone mixture.
  4. Dust the top with cocoa powder.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
